Top 5 Projects by Syist Group: Empowering Bharat Digitally

1. Matsya: India's First Short Video Streaming App with Cultural Vibes

Matsya is a proudly Made-in-India video streaming platform that brings short-form content inspired by Indian heritage, local talent, and traditional values. With a powerful backend built on Flask and SQLite, Matsya enables users to upload, view, and interact with videos in real-time.

  • Monetization for creators
  • Customizable video feeds
  • Support for regional languages
  • Focus on Indian classical music, dance & knowledge

2. Bharat Chat: Real-time Chat App for the New India

Bharat Chat is a secure, modern messaging platform built to support real-time communication for the youth and businesses of India. Developed using WebSocket protocols and Python, it features encrypted chat, media sharing, and live typing indicators.

  • End-to-end encryption
  • User profile photos, call buttons, and status updates
  • Attachment sharing & file upload feature
  • Group chats & broadcast messages

3. Rojgar Setu: Unskilled Labour Job Finder Across India

Rojgar Setu is a powerful job connectivity platform bridging the gap between unskilled workers and local employers. Built using Flask and SQLite, it allows laborers to register and find jobs near them with ease, even on low-end devices.

  • Location-based job suggestions
  • Multi-language support
  • Easy employer registration and job posting
  • Resume-less job matching algorithm

4. Syist Resume Maker: Smart Resume Builder for Everyone

Syist Resume Maker is a smart AI-powered web tool that helps users create professional resumes in minutes. With multiple templates and export options, it guides users through step-by-step form filling and generates downloadable resumes.

  • Multiple modern & traditional templates
  • Auto-suggestions for skills & achievements
  • Login/signup for resume history
  • PDF export & print-ready format

5. AI Teacher (Upcoming): Digital Gurukul of the Future

AI Teacher is an upcoming project that aims to revolutionize education through artificial intelligence. This virtual teacher will read books, split them into chapters and units, and generate unit-wise explanation videos automatically.

  • Automatic training from textbooks
  • Voice & animated video generation
  • Support for CBSE, ICSE, and State Boards
  • Ideal for rural and low-infrastructure schools
